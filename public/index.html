Malay Wordle - Katapat Game

Welcome to Malay Wordle, also known as Katapat - the exciting daily word puzzle game in Bahasa Melayu! Challenge yourself to guess the 5-letter Malay word within 6 attempts. Each day brings a new puzzle to solve.

How to play Malay Wordle: Enter any valid 5-letter Malay word and press enter. The tiles will change colors to show how close your guess was to the word. Green means the letter is correct and in the right position, yellow means the letter is in the word but in the wrong position, and gray means the letter is not in the word.

Perfect for Malaysian language learners and native speakers alike, this game helps improve vocabulary and spelling skills in Bahasa Melayu. Play Wordle Melayu unlimited - come back every day for a new challenge!
